Using the Pool During COVID-19

In order to maintain social distancing to slow the spread of COVID-19, residents must now book time in the apartment pool.

Appointment bookings allow for up to three guests for a time span of two hours

Antibacterial wipes will be provided but due to constraints, cleaning staff will not be able to disinfect surfaces between use.

Please enjoy use of the pool and follow recommended safety guidelines!

For questions, please contact the main office.
